Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and plenty of melted mozzarella. Each layer is baked to perfection, creating a comforting, cheesy, and flavorful meal that’s perfect for feeding a crowd. It's a classic comfort food that combines savory flavors and satisfying textures in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta or béchamel
  • Mozzarella & Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, Italian herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta if using regular lasagna noodles.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic, then brown the ground beef.
  3. Add tomato sauce, simmer to make the meat sauce.
  4. Layer in a baking dish: sauce, noodles, ricotta or béchamel, mozzarella. Repeat.
  5. Top with sauce and cheese.
  6. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
